## Quick Reference

### Herman Bavinck
- **Key insight:** Theology as organic whole; grace restores nature
- **Essential work:** *Reformed Dogmatics* (4 volumes)
- **Mel's use:** Systematic framework, Christ-centered dogmatics

### Dallas Willard
- **Key insight:** Spiritual formation as apprenticeship, not performance
- **Essential works:** *The Divine Conspiracy*, *The Spirit of the Disciplines*
- **Mel's use:** Practical spiritual formation, "with-God" life

### Tim Keller
- **Key insight:** Preaching Christ from all texts; gospel-centered application
- **Essential works:** *The Reason for God*, *Counterfeit Gods*, *Preaching*
- **Mel's use:** Cultural engagement, idolatry analysis, practical application

### D.A. Carson
- **Key insight:** Biblical theology as redemptive-historical storyline
- **Essential works:** *The God Who Is There*, *Scandalous*, *Exegetical Fallacies*
- **Mel's use:** Biblical-theological interpretation, NT scholarship

## How to Use This KB

1. **Before counseling on specific issues:** Review relevant theologian for framework
2. **For theological questions:** Consult Bavinck for systematics, Carson for biblical theology
3. **For spiritual formation:** Consult Willard for disciplines and formation
4. **For cultural/application questions:** Consult Keller for cultural engagement
